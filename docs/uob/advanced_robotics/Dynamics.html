Inertia and moments

In static equilibrium, relationship between end-effector force FFF and Joint torque τ\tauτ:

τ=JTF\tau = J^TF τ=JTF

Physics prequisite

force to torque:

τ=r×F=rFsin⁡θ\tau = r \times F = rF\sin\theta τ=r×F=rFsinθ

friction torque(viscous):

τ=−kθ˙\tau = -k\dot{\theta} τ=−kθ˙

弹性力（k 为弹簧的劲度系数，x 为振子偏离平衡位置的位移）：

F=−kxF = -kx F=−kx

阻尼力（c 为阻尼系数，v 为振子速度）：

F=−cvF = -cv F=−cv
